2018 Schedule

2018 involves two tracks of effort. The first is representation at races and events of Rift and its race program sponsors. I will be racing road, gravel, mountain bikes, and cyclocross, to reach maximum exposure and put forth one of the Rift brand values that specialization in the bike world is unneccessary. The second effort is actual apparel design, production, and sales. Both are outlined below, and subject to tweaks that do not alter the overall direction. Roughly half of my races I will be targeting podiums or wins, and the other half will be Elite- or UCI- level races where brand representation will be enhanced.
